# PP-01.01 - Platform To Runtime Directionality

**COD standard status:** Official and mandatory Clean Onion Documentation standard.

## Policy

Platform policies in Layer 5 must map inward to runtime policies in Layer 3.

## Mandatory rules

- Every effective platform policy must map to at least one `RP-XXX` artifact in Layer 3.
- Runtime policies must not declare upward normative dependency on Layer 5 policy files.
- Mapping ownership is maintained in `runtime.time.md` within the policy type folder.

## Compliance notes

- `PP-XX.YY` ids are stable references for governance audits.
- Missing or broken `RP-XXX` links for effective policies are non-compliant.
