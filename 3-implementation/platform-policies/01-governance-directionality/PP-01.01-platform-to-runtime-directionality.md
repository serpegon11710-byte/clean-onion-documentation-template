# PP-01.01 - Platform To Runtime Directionality

**COD standard status:** Official and mandatory product policy standard at Layer 3.

## Policy

Runtime policies in Layer 3 technology folders must map to platform policies in Layer 3 root.

## Mandatory rules

- Every effective `RP-XXX` artifact must map to at least one `PP-XX.YY` artifact.
- RP to PP mapping ownership is maintained in `rp-to-pp-matrix.md` within the technology folder that owns the runtime policies.
- Platform policy definitions remain technology-agnostic and live under `3-implementation/platform-policies/`.
- Layer 3 compliance evidence for RP traceability must be verifiable from Layer 3 artifacts only.

## Compliance notes

- `PP-XX.YY` ids are stable references for product-policy audits.
- Missing or broken RP to PP links for effective runtime policies are non-compliant.
